Copyright Holders | Licensors

Composers | Songwriters | Music Producers

Producers of music and authors of text are entitled to copyright protection. Every original work is an intellectual property. We help artists with all licensing aspects without breaking the creative flow.

Learn more

Copyright Users | Licensees

Film Producers | Content Creators | Event Organizers

Including music in advertisements, film projects or playing music on public events obligates licensees to observe intellectual property laws and copyright regulations. These are made to protect music producers and their artwork. We support licensees with our highly specialized services.

Find out how

Technical Providers

Music Streaming | Video on Demand | Social Media Platforms

Technical content providers represent a sub-category of copyright users. They don't use copyrighted works themselves but provide access to a broad audience. Therefore they are bound by copyright laws as licensees. This requires dedicated licensing and accounting solutions which we can provide.

Our services

Copyright Collectives

Mechanical Rights Organisations | Performance Rights Organisations

Copyright Societies license musical artwork on behalf of the copyright holders. They ensure that license fees are paid for the use of music, either in publications or performances.
